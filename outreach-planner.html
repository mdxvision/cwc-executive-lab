Outreach Templates for Reference

To a Potential Mentor (Cold Outreach)

"Hi [Name], I've followed your work at [Company] and was particularly inspired by [specific accomplishment]. As a [your role] working toward [aspiration], I'd love to learn from your experience. Would you be open to a 20-minute call? I'd be grateful for any insights you'd share."

To an Internal Senior Leader

"Hi [Name], I'm [Your Name] from [Team]. I've admired how you [specific observation] and would value learning from your perspective on [topic]. Would you have 15 minutes for coffee? I'm developing my skills in [area] and your experience would be incredibly helpful."

To a Potential Peer Ally

"Hi [Name], I've noticed we're both navigating [similar challenge]. I'm building a small group of peers for mutual support and idea-sharing. Would you be interested in connecting? I think we could learn a lot from each other."
