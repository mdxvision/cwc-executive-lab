How to Use This Planner

Different contexts call for different presence strategies. Use this worksheet to define how you want to show up in your most important professional settings, and the specific behaviors that will help you achieve that impact.

Example: Executive Team Meeting

Context: Weekly executive team meeting with CEO and peers

Desired Impression: Strategic thinker, confident voice, equal contributor

Gravitas: Sit at the table (not back row), maintain eye contact with CEO during my points

Communication: Lead with recommendation, use "I recommend..." not "I think maybe..."

Appearance: Dress at executive level, wear my signature color to be memorable
