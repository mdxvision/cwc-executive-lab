Step 2: Review Key Techniques

The Early Entry

Contribute in the first 10 minutes to establish your voice and stake your position before the conversation gets crowded.

"Before we go further, I want to add that..." or "Building on what [Name] said, I'd suggest..."

The Confident Pause

When interrupted, pause briefly, maintain eye contact, and continue where you left off or redirect the conversation.

"I'd like to finish my thought..." [pause] "As I was saying, the data shows..."

The Reclaim

When someone repeats your idea without credit, acknowledge it graciously while clearly establishing ownership.

"I'm glad that resonates, [Name]. When I suggested it earlier, my thinking was..."

The Coalition Build

Before key meetings, connect 1:1 with stakeholders to share your perspective and understand their priorities.

"I wanted to get your thoughts before Thursday's meeting. Here's what I'm thinking..."
