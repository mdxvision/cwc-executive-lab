Tips for Great Testimonials

  • Record on Zoom or have them send selfie videos
  • Authentic > polished - raw emotion converts
  • Specifics beat generalities ("I got promoted" > "I feel more confident")
  • Keep clips to 15-30 seconds for social media use

General Recording Tips

Delivery:

  • Look directly into the camera lens (not the screen)
  • Speak slightly slower than feels natural
  • Pause after important points - let them land
  • Show emotion - passion converts
  • It's okay to stumble - authenticity > perfection

Technical:

  • Natural lighting facing you (window light is great)
  • Phone at eye level (stack books if needed)
  • Quiet space with minimal background noise
  • Vertical format for Reels/TikTok, horizontal for LinkedIn/YouTube

Posting Strategy:

  • Post different versions across platforms
  • Repurpose longer content into shorter clips
  • Add captions - most people watch without sound
  • Include a clear CTA in every post
